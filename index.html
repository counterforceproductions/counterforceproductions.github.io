Our game features

Our game is really fun since it features realistic weapons and have actual war vehicles. The aim of this game is to capture a point. One team attacks one defends.There are also a variety of different game modes such as capture the flag and king of the hill. There are tanks, jeeps even rocket launchers. Our game is sure to please you.

More Game Info

Our game will be available on Steam since it is a well known gaming platform. It will just be for PC.

Our game will sadly not have multiplayer online but you can manually have 2 players. It will also feature realistic transportation vehicles and weapons.

There will be military like vehicles such as submarines, fighter jets as well as tanks. I am sure this game will please many shooter fanatics and will be a very good game.
